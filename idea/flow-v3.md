# Push or Pay — MVP User Flow v3 (authoritative)

Brand personality: **"A funny game I play with my wife."** Not a punishment system.
The streak creates the habit. The spouse interaction creates the magic.

Voice: fun, playful, LOVING. Never guilt. Money is the joke; streak is the hero.

## Language
- REMOVE (negative): Promise, Accountability Partner, Penalty, Failure, Free.
- USE: Challenge, Final Boss / Watcher, **Lazy Tax** 💸, Skip Day.

## Flow
1. Warning / Landing — "Do NOT show this app to your spouse… unless you're serious about building habits." [I understand the risk] → toast "your excuses have been notified 😈".
2. Create Your Challenge 😈 — 💪 Daily Push-ups, target reps, Lazy Tax 💸 (default 🇮🇩 Rp 10,000 — sounds scary, ≈US$0.64).
3. Invite — "Challenge created 😈 Now your wife gets to watch. [Send Invite]" → "There is no turning back now 😈". (The invite itself is the fun moment — NO forced reveal screen.)
4. Wife Invitation Page — "😈 You've been invited. Sam is trying to build a habit. Daily challenge: 10 push-ups. If he skips: you get Rp 10,000. Your mission: keep watching 👀 [Accept Challenge]".
5. Wife Accepted — "You're in 😈 You can now: see his streak, watch progress, raise his Lazy Tax. Good luck, Sam."
6. Wife Controls Lazy Tax — current, [Double] [5×] [Custom] → owner banner "🚨 Your wife raised your Lazy Tax Rp10,000 → Rp50,000. She believes in you. Probably."
7. Main Dashboard — order: **Streak → Challenge → Heatmap → Money(Lazy Tax)**. "🔥 Day 12. Your streak is alive."
8. Streak tracker — GitHub heatmap; tap a square → day detail (reps, duration).
9. Push-up Mode — counter, tap with chin, timer, [Complete].
10. Completed — "🔥 Streak Protected! Day 13. 23 push-ups. Duration 58s. Your wife earned Rp0 today 😂".
11. Skip Day — no guilt. "Tough day. Winner: your wife 🏆".
12. Share Card — "😭 My wife earned Rp60,000 this week… time to get stronger." / "My wife discovered passive income. Unfortunately, it's my laziness."
